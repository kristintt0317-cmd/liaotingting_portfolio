Research / Experiments

Research-led, technical, and exploratory work.

This section gathers projects that are stronger when framed as research, prototyping, or experimentation rather than conventional design case studies.

Chinese Sign Language Recognition Interaction System

Built between Jan 2026 and Mar 2026, this project focused on translating recognition capability into a usable communication-support interaction. The prototype used PyTorch, CNN + LSTM, and OpenCV to support data handling, training, and real-time camera demonstration.

Focus: interaction flow, model testing, prototype iteration, real-world generalisation issues.

Subtle-Signal Mediator Prototype

Developed between Oct 2025 and Mar 2026, this proof-of-concept prototype explored how AI could play a low-intrusion mediating role in communication. Python, OpenCV, and MediaPipe were used to prompt, record, and revisit subtle cues within interaction.

Focus: problem definition, interaction logic, concept validation, AI-assisted communication.

Express the Unexpressed

A theory-led research direction investigating subtle signals, mediation, and human–robot interaction. This work is best presented as an editorial or critical-design research page rather than a standard product case study.

Focus: HRI, critical design, communication mediation, theory-driven framing.

Generative Mechanical Score

A completed creative-coding project that turns mechanical noise into a visual audio agent through feature extraction, Pygame-based motion, and sound-driven visual behaviour.
